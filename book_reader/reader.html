To add bookmarks, rightclick or rightclick and drag on the text. Then, doubleclick on a bookmark to remove it. Your bookmarks are saved when you switch to another e-book or when you click on the save button. To scroll directly to a bookmark, click on the next botton. To change bookmark color, click on the color button.

E-Book Reader DEMO

This is a project requested by a friend. I had a few experience in HTML, CSS and Javascript, and barely started looking into JQuery at the time when this was created. Little did I know, I need a server to load and upload documents (books)...

So this app is free to download and used locally on your own machine!

Note: it might not work well on FireFox (and possible some other browsers).

HOW TO USE

  1. right-click and drag to create a bookmark
  2. double-click on a bookmark to remove it
  3. there a buttons on the menu to help you save bookmarks, locate bookmarks and change bookmark color
