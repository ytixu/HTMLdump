Story

Deep down of the underworld closest to Hell, there exists a secret chamber of area no larger than an average bedroom, where young students entertain their teachers with exotic arts. This room (commonly named as the "RBC playhouse") is pierced at the center by a thick metallic pole that could look shinier with richer light equipment. Due to the relatively high intellectual-minded customers that attend regularly, the place becomes the sacred wonderland of discoveries and enlightenment. It is believed that many globally recognized NP-hard problems were reduced to NP-less-hard there, whenever a certain young student does his performance - which is eventually known as the bar dancer's effect. As time goes by, the name of that young student's name sank into oblivion, as well as the other glorious stories of the beginning of NP-less-hardness (which is now proven to be the exact same set as EXPSPACE). Yet, the spirits of ambition and passion still reside the room: especially the eight legendary philosophers, who from time to time, come in to plug themselves at the table around the pole, and think about the greater questions beyond the universe.
